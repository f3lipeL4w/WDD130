Home page

Having a great father is indeed very wholesome, be ready to learn about this awesome individual.

Years ago my father left his home north of the border and went all the way to the southern mountains of Brazil.

The purpose of this site is to show off my father and his traits. I want to show how he is a great father, husband, and friend. I also want to show how he is a great person in general.
